About

What is IDaH?

Interactive History of Denmark (IDaH) is a platform that gamifies historical topics — from the oldest Danish bicycle to early aviation, Viking logistics, and more. Each module can run in a web browser as a lightweight experience or as a richer downloadable version for museums and classrooms.

Fast to try

Click and play in the browser — no installs for the lightweight tier.

Classroom‑ready

Teachers can assemble task lists, track scores, and spark friendly competition.

Museum‑friendly

Local, curated builds for on‑site kiosks — with sponsorship credits.

Experiences

Playable history, built to scale

Pick from lightweight browser demos or full simulations. Here are example “cards” you can edit or duplicate.

Velocipede Race (2D Silhouette)

A stylish, silhouette‑driven race with rhythm‑based pedaling. Classroom‑friendly and presentation‑ready.

First Danish Military Plane — “Glenten”

Flight sim showcasing early aviation. Browser teaser; full HDRP build for museum installs.

Uroksen fra Vig

Track and hunt the prehistoric aurochs with limited tools. Emphasis on realism and restraint.

Classroom

Make history competitive (in a good way)

Teachers can build a “task list” of experiences, add questions, and award points for speed, accuracy, and repetition — the “Hugekko” principle: Hurtig, Gentagende, Korrekt.

  • Private classroom links
  • Automatic scoreboards
  • Exportable results for grading
